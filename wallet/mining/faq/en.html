1. First, top up your miner balance, then select a tariff and the token domain you want to mine.

2. The mining probability is calculated based on the current difficulty of mining the selected coin.

3. If the miner successfully finds a token block, a network commission of $0.0003 must be paid to receive the token.

4. After completing the task, the mined tokens are credited to the miner's balance.

5. To avoid spam, click the "Withdraw" button every minute to transfer the tokens to your account.
