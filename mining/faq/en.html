1. First, purchase energy for the miner. 100 energy = $1

2. The mining probability depends on the current mining difficulty of the selected coin.

3. If the miner finds a token block, a network fee of $0.0003 must be paid to claim it.

4. After a successful operation, the mined tokens are credited to the miner's balance.

5. To prevent spam, click the "Withdraw" button every minute to transfer the tokens to your account.
