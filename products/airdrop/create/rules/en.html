Airdrop Rules

  1. The creator of the airdrop cannot participate in the contest.
  2. Participants must have a valid wallet.
  3. Each participant can participate only once.
  4. Using bots to participate in the airdrop is prohibited.
  5. Participants must follow all instructions specified in the airdrop.
  6. Rewards will be distributed within 30 days after the airdrop ends.
  7. Organizers reserve the right to change the rules at any time.
  8. Any attempts at fraud will result in disqualification.
